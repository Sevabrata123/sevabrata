Background

Sevabrata is a non-profit and apolitical social organization that has been conceived to extend service to the needy people, who look for medical guidance, related financial assistance along with logistic and psychological support. A group of members across globe, inspired by the idea of service to mankind, had already undertaken such initiatives for extremely needy people. Success of these activities has inspired us to form an organization to continue with these noble services in a more organized way
